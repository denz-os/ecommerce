Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions


Is LinkUP free to use? ⌄
Yes! Creating an account, browsing listings and even listing your own items is completely free. There are no hidden fees or commissions taken from your sales.
How do I know if a seller is trustworthy? ⌄
Every seller on LinkUP has a public profile with ratings and reviews from previous buyers. You can also view their listing history and chat with them before committing to anything.
Can I sell as a small business or informal trader? ⌄
Absolutely! LinkUP is perfect for small businesses, side hustles and informal traders. You can create multiple listings and reach customers across your city or even nationwide.
How does payment and delivery work? ⌄
Payment and delivery are arranged directly between the buyer and seller via our chat feature. This keeps things flexible — cash, EFT, or meet-up arrangements are all up to you.
What if I have a problem with a listing or user? ⌄
You can flag any listing or user directly from the platform. Our moderation team reviews reports and takes action to keep the community safe and trustworthy.
