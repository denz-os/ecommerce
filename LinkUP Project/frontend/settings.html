Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a product on LinkUP? ▾
Browse products on the marketplace, click a listing to view it, then click "Add to Cart". Go to your cart, complete the checkout steps — delivery details, payment method, and review — then confirm your order.
How do I list a product for sale? ▾
Go to the Sell page from the navigation bar. Fill in your product name, price, category, condition, location and description. Upload up to 6 product photos. Click Publish to make your listing live.
How do promo codes work? ▾
When you are on the cart page, enter a valid promo code in the "Promo code" field in the order summary and click Apply. Try LINKUP10 for 10% off, WELCOME20 for 20% off, or SAVE15 for 15% off.
What is the refund policy? ▾
If your item is not as described or does not arrive, contact the seller through chat first. If unresolved within 48 hours, open a dispute via the Help Centre and our team will assist within 3 working days.
How do I report a seller or listing? ▾
On any product listing, click the "Report" button at the bottom. Alternatively, submit a support message here with the subject "Report a User" and include the seller's name and listing details.
